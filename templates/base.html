Instructions
Generate a wordcloud of your WhatsApp chats easily to get a summary of the topics you've talked about.

*No chats are stored in the server, the wordcloud however is stored and publicly accessible at /static/WordCloud.png*

How to use

  • Export your chat using the WhatsApp app on your mobile by going to the options on top right > Export chat
  • Click on 'Browse' and upload this chat file
  • Apply the filters for the dates, which will be considered to select the messages only between that specified range
  • Click on 'Generate'
